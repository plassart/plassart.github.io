About me

I am currently a Post-doc researcher in LCA2 laboratory at EPFL. My current research focuses on network calculus. My research interests are also in optimization, Markov decision process, learning, energy, and real-time systems. I hold a PhD from Grenoble Alpes University entitled “online energy optimization on real-time system”. This Phd was done in POLARIS and SPADES teams at LIG and at Inria Grenoble. My advisors were Bruno Gaujal and Alain Girault. This thesis was included in the CASERM project.
